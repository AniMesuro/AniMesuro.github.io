Commission

These are the base prices I ask for personal commissions. Some of the info may not apply for bigger projects, in which some negotiation might be necessary.

- For commercial projects the price might vary depending on project popularity, length of time it's used, etc.
- For a project that requires a series of drawings, there's a discount. For every five illustrations, one of them is free.

- Additional characters cost 0.5x more from the artwork value.
- Backgrounds may range in price. Transparent and flat background is free.
- Complex designs might vary the price.
